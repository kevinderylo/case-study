Case study with google lens and mobilenet


This is a case study between Google lens and mobilenet:


In the 1st test with the pencil google lens suceeded by guessing right while mobilenet guessed spotlight.




In the 2nd test with the pill-bottle google lens suceeded by guessing right while mobilenet guessed punching bag.




In the 3rd test with the aloe google lens suceeded by guessing right while mobilenet guessed seatbelt.




In the 4th test with the i-phone google lens suceeded by guessing right while mobilenet guessed meat-clever.




In the 5h test with the modal car google lens suceeded by guessing right while mobilenet guessed blow dryer.
